Product Testing

Functional performance testing is a critical element of the product development life cycle; both during development and throughout life cycle management. Depending on the stage of development, testing needs vary: characterize competitive product, feasibility of early prototypes; design verification testing; comparative testing to predicate devices in support of regulatory submissions; monitoring product performance throughout aging studies (accelerated and real-time).

Common Questions EdgeOne Helps Solve
  • Struggling to identify the best method to test your medical device?
  • Lacking resources (FTE and/or Eqpt) necessary to conduct the testing in-house?
  • Small start-up looking for testing support on an as needed basis?
  • Large company needing auxiliary support for your existing testing/engineering lab?
Key Offerings
  • Device Characterization for Client, Competitor, or Targeted-Acquistion Devices)
  • Early Prototype Feasibility Testing
  • Design Verification Testing (per existing product requirements)
  • Testing per ISO/ASTM Standards
  • Testing to support Regulatory Submission(s) including Predicate Device
  • Test Method Development
How We Do It
  • Testing governed by ISO 13845 quality system
  • Work collaboratively with client to understand need and scope of test
  • Develop test method (if necessary) and validate
  • Execute testing per EdgeOne GDP and GLP
  • Conduct necessary data analysis
  • Final report with quality review and customer concurrence
