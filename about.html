The Company

EdgeOne Medical, a trusted partner of medical device firms, is focused on providing product realization services for the development and life cycle management of devices through comprehensive product performance testing, quality and regulatory services.

EdgeOne Medical was founded in 2012 by a team of experienced medical device development professionals with a successful track record of leading, launching and maintaining a diverse portfolio of medical devices in the global marketplace. They recognized a market need for a premier testing service organization capable of conducting a wide range of testing (from the simple to the complex) while also having the ability to correlate the testing needs to the appropriate context of the product life cycle.

Since product performance testing on a medical device is often driven by a quality and/or regulatory requirement, it was only natural to incorporate quality and regulatory services into EdgeOne's portfolio.   

This unique combination of an experienced medical device management team and a complementary array of testing, quality and regulatory services means we are ideally suited to support all types of firms and a diverse array of medical device projects/products.

By having been in your shoes, we can help you achieve your medical device realization goals!
