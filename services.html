Services Overview

The business needs of the medical device developer and manufacturer are constantly in flux and influenced by any of the following: regulations change, global socio-economic conditions, financial constraints, competitive offerings, product complaints and audit findings. You require an effective partner in the medical device industry capable of dealing with a diverse and growing array of challenges.

EdgeOne Medical is a service provider focused on providing product performance testing, regulatory and quality support for medical devices in development or already on the market.  EdgeOne has the flexibility and necessary capabilities to adapt to your specific needs by offering a single point of service for a focused task or by providing a more integrated offering leveraging some or all of our core services in quality, regulatory and testing.

If your specific needs require support beyond quality, regulatory and/or testing, EdgeOne Medical is still an option. Due to our experience in the medical device industry and extensive network, we have the capability to coordinate and offer a fully integrated and comprehensive service offering that includes amongst other things design and/or manufacturing.
