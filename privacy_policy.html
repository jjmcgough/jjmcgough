Policies

EdgeOne Medical makes it a priority to respect and protect your privacy. Therefore, the information you provide in accessing our online tools and resources is gathered in a secure environment.

What Information Do We Gather?

EdgeOne Medical gathers information from two sources on this site: personal data (such as names and e-mail addresses); and aggregated data (such as the use of software to track and monitor users). The personal data we gather may include the following:

-Name

-Company

-Address

-City

-State

-Postal Code

-Country

-Email Address

Like many other sites, EdgeOne Medical uses software to automatically track and monitor each visitor’s domain name, browser type, date and time of access, as well as other information. EdgeOne Medical does not associate this tracking data with any specific users who browse the Site, nor do we distribute any data to third parties.

EdgeOne Medical and its affiliates will apply adequate privacy safeguards to protect such personal information.
