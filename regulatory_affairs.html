Regulatory

From early-stage to large, global companies, we can help you understand the regulatory landscape for your medical device and provide a fully integrated approach to device development and effective communications with Regulatory Authorities.

EdgeOne Medical can help you with submissions, responding to Regulatory Authority deficiencies, or developing/submitting a multi-country strategy. We have the expertise you need for product realization and post market activities.

Common Questions EdgeOne helps solve:

1. Do you need help identifying your product classification per FDA's QSR or EU (Medical Device Directive 93/42/EEC)?

2. Have you identified the predicate device(s) for your 510k?

3. Is your device considered a combination product?
