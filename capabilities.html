CapabilitiesThe essence of what we do

Engineering Analysis

Testing may not always lead to passing results. When such a situation occurs, EdgeOne Medical is capable of offering the appropriate level of design analyses in order to determine root cause of the failure as well as provide suggestions on design improvements/modifications.

Project Management

At EdgeOne we understand that a key to successful outcomes is providing management of team activities through productive, focused team meetings with schedule tracking and action item accountability. All projects are governed by core project management principles in order to maintain focus on successful project execution.

Strategic Product Development Consulting

Given our extensive experience in the medical device industry, EdgeOne can support your strategic product development planning efforts.

> For an existing portfolio of products, we can develop a technical and/or global expansion strategy that would result in next generation products (new/improved features and enhancements).

> For pipeline projects or ideas, we can evaluate and provide a full product analysis including technical risk assessment, estimated project timelines and budgets, regulatory and manufacturing strategies. Life Cycle Management (LCM)

Often companies believe that regulatory approval is the end of device development, but in fact, this is only the beginning. Many issues arise once your product is commercialized and the necessary impact assessment, design change, and documentation must follow.  EdgeOne specializes in supporting your product’s entire lifecycle, from product realization through post-market support (a.ka. Life Cycle Management (LCM)).

1. Have you received a supplier change notice for a critical device component and need to repeat verification testing with a new material?

2. Are you currently dealing with a product recall?  Do you need help developing a comprehensive strategy and action plan to address the issue?

3. Are you currently experiencing an increased number of complaints?  Have you identified the root cause?  Do you need help understanding and documenting the associated quality system outputs such as CAPA, Change Control and Risk Management File?
