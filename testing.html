Testing

Functional performance testing is a critical element of the product development life cycle; both during development and throughout life cycle managment. Depending on the stage of development, testing needs vary: characterize competitive product, characterize early prototypes; design verification testing; comparative testing to predicate devices in support of regulatory submissions; monitor product performance per aging studies (accelerated and real-time).

Common Questions EdgeOne Helps Solve:

>Struggling to identify the best method to test your medical device?

>Lacking resources (FTE and/or Eqpt) necessary to conduct the testing in-house?

>Small start-up looking for testing support on an as needed basis?

Key Offerings:

>Device Characterization (Client or Competitors)

>Early Prototype Design Feasibility Testing

>Testing to Existing ISO/ASTM Standards

>Testing to Support Regulatory Submission including Predicate Device Testing

>Test Method Development
